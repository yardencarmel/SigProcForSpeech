PHASE 5 — Noise-Injection Style Transfer (Improvement 2)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[V] 5.1  Implement noise-injection inference (run_noise_inject.py)
         x_0 = (1 - alpha) * randn + alpha * mel_B
