Phase 5 — Result Plots
======================

Generated by: scripts/run_all_phases.py --phases 5

sway_sampling.png
  WER (%) and SIM-o vs Sway Sampling coefficient s, with one line per NFE
  setting (8, 16, 32 function evaluations). Reproduces Figure 3 of the
  F5-TTS paper. Shows that s=-0.8 or s=-1.0 with NFE >= 16 achieves near-
  zero WER on the test sentences.

cfg_strength.png
  WER (%) and SIM-o vs CFG strength (1.0 to 3.0). Confirms the paper's
  recommended default of cfg=2.0 as a good balance between text faithfulness
  and speaker identity preservation.

emotion_weight.png
  SIM-o and MCD vs emotion blend weight (0.0 to 1.0) for Phase 4 v1 results.
  Note: v1 had Vocos inversion failures at intermediate weights; the
  monotonic trends are cleaner in the Phase 4 (direct injection) results.
  See results/emotion_transfer/emotion_transfer.png for the v2 plot.

sway_pdf.png
  ODE timestep probability density pi(t) for each Sway Sampling coefficient.
  Visualises how negative s concentrates integration steps near t=0 (the
  noisy end of the flow trajectory), which is why it helps quality without
  increasing NFE.
